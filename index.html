¿Por qué aprender a programar?

Si aún te haces esta pregunta...

Our way of working, of communicating, shopping and even our way of thinking have changed tremendously in the last decade. Actually, our whole world is in a process of transformation that is due to the technological revolution in which we are involved ... and it will be even greater in the coming years.

In this situation, learn programming and develop computational thinking provides us with the necessary skills to understand the way in which the digital world works, and allows us to explore new ideas and build new solutions for both work and leisure. And what's more important, learning of this new universal language develops our creativity, offers us new ways of communication and gives us the possibility of working together with wonderful people from all over the world.

  • Familias

Niños, jóvenes y adultos

Children, youth and adults can participate in workshops programming or even organize their own events to show other things that can be created by programming.

Programadores

Programmers can organize workshops programming in schools, associations, cultural centers ...

Docentes

Teachers can prepare scheduled programming classes, sharing their teaching materials or other teachers explain how they use their programming classes to work in other disciplines.

Teachers can not programmed seminars and invite parents or mothers who program, or other students who already know how to program, to learn from each other.

Familias

Families can encourage their children to participate in these workshops and even participate together in any of them.What better way to learn to do it in family!

Companies and organizations

Companies and organizations can hold events, to allow their staff to go to events to act as mentors, creating fun challenges of programming or sponsor events.
